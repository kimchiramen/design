About

This Time Tracking Progressive Web App can:

  • Capture hours against projects/positions/roles.
  • Create and manage multi-level positions and roles in projects.
  • Define project milestones, sets up budgets and other metrics that need to be tracked.
  • Capture project status in real-time and get instant visibility into project costs and performance.

Ideation & Concept

I started with sketches to communicate my ideas, and also to think about the layout of the product. Then I continued with a high-fidelity wireframes in Adobe XD based on my sketches.
